# 1D

11-15, 2+!d.  Not suitable for 1N (13-15).  No 5M unless 6!d.  Not suitable
for 2C (5c4M with good clubs or 6+c).  Announce: "Could be short".

No jumps by opener are forcing; responder is captain since opener is limited
to 15.

Responses:
