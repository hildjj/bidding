Examples¶

To play¶

1N-(2♥)-2♠: Let's play 2♠ please.

1N-(2♠)-2N!-(P)-3♣!-(P)-3D: Let's play 3♦, please.

Invite+¶

1N-(2♥)-3♣!: I invite+ you to game, with 5+ diamonds. Bid 3♦ to say you have a minimum. I will bid on if I have enough for game.

1N-(2♥)-3♦!: I invite+ you to game, with 5+ spades (transfer *through* the opponent's heart suit). Bid 3♠ to say you have a minimum. I will bid on if I have enough for game.

Stayman¶

1N-(2♥)-3♥!: Do you have 4S? I don't have a heart stopper.

1N-(2♥)-2N!-(P)-3♣!-(P)-3♥!: Do you have 4S? I have a heart stopper. Want to play 3N or 4♠?

Direct to 3N¶

1N-(2♥)-3N!: I don't have a heart stopper.

1N-(2♥)-2N!-(P)-3♣!-(P)-3N!: I have a heart stopper.
