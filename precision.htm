Overcalls

DONT

I like bidding over 1NT and strong 1♣ systems, at the beginner to intermediate level. Many partnerships at that level haven't discussed what to do over any interference, including a double. At a higher level, there's lots of interference, so everyone has a plan. DONT may or may not be the right choice there, but it's a contender at least.

Over 1♣, shift all of the responses down.
